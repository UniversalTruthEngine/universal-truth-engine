# Epistemic Analytics Layer v1

## Purpose

The Epistemic Analytics Layer allows the Universal Truth Engine to begin analysing its own structure.

UTE should not only store truths and display them. It should identify where the knowledge architecture is strong, weak, incomplete, over-centralised, or under-validated.

---

## Core Idea

A truth graph can reveal properties that are not obvious from individual entries.

Examples:

- Which truths are most foundational?
- Which truths are bridge nodes between domains?
- Which truths are highly depended upon but weakly proven?
- Which dependencies are missing?
- Which truths are isolated?
- Which clusters are underdeveloped?
- Which concepts may be duplicated or confused?

---

## First Analytics Categories

### 1. Dependency Centrality

Measures how many other truths directly depend on a truth.

High centrality suggests epistemic mass.

### 2. Recursive Dependency Centrality

Measures how many truths depend on a truth directly or indirectly.

This better represents deep foundational importance.

### 3. Bridge Score

Measures whether a truth connects multiple domains.

Bridge truths are important for navigation and interdisciplinary reconstruction.

### 4. Proof Completeness

A provisional measure of whether a truth has:

- entry file,
- proof file,
- metadata,
- reconstruction section,
- conditions of validity,
- failure cases,
- review history.

### 5. Missing Dependency Detection

Identifies dependencies that point to non-existent truth IDs.

### 6. Orphan Detection

Identifies truths with no dependencies and no dependents.

These may be root primitives, or they may be disconnected entries.

### 7. High-Centrality Weak-Proof Risk

Identifies truths that many other truths depend on but whose proof layer is incomplete.

These are epistemic risk points.

---

## Why This Matters

UTE should eventually be able to guide its own improvement.

The topology should reveal:

```text
where the foundations are strong
where the foundations are weak
where the map has gaps
where new truths are needed
```
