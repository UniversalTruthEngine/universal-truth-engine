# Introduction to the Universal Truth Engine

The Universal Truth Engine (UTE) is an attempt to build a navigable structured universe of foundational knowledge.

Rather than treating knowledge as isolated documents or webpages, UTE treats truths as interconnected objects with:
- dependencies,
- proofs,
- confidence levels,
- reconstruction pathways,
- and machine-readable structure.

UTE combines:
- knowledge preservation,
- proof architecture,
- topology generation,
- and epistemic analytics
into a single evolving system.

The system is intended for:
- humans,
- AI systems,
- future reconstruction,
- education,
- and long-term knowledge integrity.
