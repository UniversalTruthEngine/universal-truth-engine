# Origin Statement

The Universal Truth Engine concept was originated by John David Stiles.

This includes the broader idea of a structured Fact Vault and a navigable cosmological map of fundamental knowledge: a truth-space where facts, proofs, dependencies, assumptions, and reconstruction pathways can be explored by humans, future artificial intelligence systems, and other intelligent beings.

This file is intended to preserve origin attribution as part of the project's historical record.

## Foundational Intention

The purpose of the Universal Truth Engine is not merely to collect facts, but to preserve the structure of reliable knowledge:

- what is known,
- how it is known,
- how it can be re-derived,
- what it depends on,
- when it fails,
- and how future intelligence may reconstruct it.
