# Derivation — UTE-FV-0002

Equality is foundational and is often treated as axiomatic in formal systems.

It may also be operationally demonstrated through one-to-one correspondence.

## Minimal Demonstration

Take two collections:

```text
Collection A: stone stone stone
Collection B: mark  mark  mark
```

Pair each stone with exactly one mark.

If every stone pairs with one mark and every mark pairs with one stone, the collections are equal in number.

This demonstrates equality of quantity without requiring writing, advanced mathematics, or institutional authority.
