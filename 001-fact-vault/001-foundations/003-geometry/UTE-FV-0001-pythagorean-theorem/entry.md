# UTE-FV-0001 — Pythagorean Theorem

## Claim

For any right triangle in a flat Euclidean plane:

```text
a^2 + b^2 = c^2
```

where `a` and `b` are the perpendicular side lengths and `c` is the hypotenuse.

## Confidence Level

Level 5 — Mathematical / Logical Proof

## Conditions of Validity

- Euclidean geometry
- Flat space
- Right triangle
- Consistent unit measurement

## Failure Cases

- Non-right triangles
- Curved surfaces
- Non-Euclidean geometries
- Measurement error in physical demonstrations

## Applications

- Construction
- Surveying
- Navigation
- Engineering
- Coordinate geometry
- Physics
- Computer graphics

## Preservation Priority

Critical foundational geometry.
