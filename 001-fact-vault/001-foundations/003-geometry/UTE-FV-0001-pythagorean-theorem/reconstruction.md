# Reconstruction — UTE-FV-0001

## Minimum Tools

- Rope or cord
- Flat surface
- Marking tool
- Measuring unit, optional

## Practical Rediscovery Method

1. Mark a rope into 12 equal segments.
2. Form a triangle with side lengths 3, 4, and 5 segments.
3. Observe that the angle between sides 3 and 4 is a right angle.
4. Verify:

```text
3^2 + 4^2 = 5^2
9 + 16 = 25
```

5. Test additional right triangles.
6. Generalise using area proof.
