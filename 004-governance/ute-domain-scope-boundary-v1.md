# UTE Domain Scope Boundary v1

## Purpose
Define the formal scope of UTE Phase 1 development.

## In Scope
- Logic
- Mathematics
- Arithmetic
- Measurement
- Geometry
- Computation
- Scientific Reconstruction

## Out of Scope (For Now)
- Ethics
- Politics
- Aesthetics
- Tacit Knowledge
- Social Constructs
- Institutional Truths

## Governance Principle
UTE should first prove itself in domains where reconstruction, proof, and dependency analysis are strongest.
