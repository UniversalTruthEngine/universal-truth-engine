# Human + LLM Guided Derivation Protocol v1

## Purpose

Define how UTE may propose new truths from existing truths without becoming self-authorising.

## Core Principle

UTE may derive candidates.

Humans and LLMs may review candidates.

Only reviewed candidates may become Core Truths.

---

## Derivation Flow

```text
Stabilised truths
    ↓
Truth Frontier Engine
    ↓
Candidate truths
    ↓
Human + LLM review
    ↓
Adversarial review
    ↓
Assurance + Soundness Review
    ↓
Fact Vault admission
```

---

## Roles

### UTE

Identifies enabled candidate truths from existing topology.

### LLMs

Help explain, test, prove, critique, and refine candidates.

### Humans

Make final admission decisions and resolve judgement questions.

---

## Candidate Statuses

- candidate
- under_review
- accepted
- stabilised
- rejected
- superseded

---

## Safety Rule

A candidate truth must never be treated as accepted simply because it was generated by the system.

Derivation proposes.

Review decides.
