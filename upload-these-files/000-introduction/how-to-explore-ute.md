# How to Explore UTE

## Purpose

The Universal Truth Engine (UTE) is designed as a navigable universe of interconnected Core Truths.

The interactive cosmos map is the primary exploration interface.

Open:

https://universaltruthengine.github.io/universal-truth-engine/

Click nodes to inspect:
- Summary
- Proof
- Dependencies
- Raw Proof

UTE attempts to preserve:
- dependency structures,
- proof chains,
- logical relationships,
- and reconstructable foundations for both humans and machines.
